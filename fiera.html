client

Fiera Ceramics

Fiera

Brand identity project for Fiera Ceramics, an independent firm of handmade pottery pieces from Madrid.

A design system was developed from which UX/UI design proposals for a website were developed, as well as different marketing applications.

logos

The word fiera comes from the Latin fera and means beast, wild animal. The name comes from understanding the artisan process as something experimental, wild and unpredictable, resulting in a Fiera, an object with personality and strong character. These attributes are the ones that inspire the brand's logo.
